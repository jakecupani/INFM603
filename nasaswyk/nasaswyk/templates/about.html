About

Nasa prides itself of creating technological breakthroughs and expanding our understanding of various topics of scientific research. However they also want to create resources to deliver to younger audiences and educators to learn and build a foundation based in STEM. Increasing the involvement and exposure to STEM fields in K-12 environments will enhance higher education, strengthen online education, and introduce students to a diverse set of skills. We encourage a collaborative effort with students, educators, and parents to not only make learning more engaging, but to prepare generations as they pave the way to a new era of innovation.


For Teachers

For our educators, we want to provide the most engaging tools and resources for your students. Keeping your students engaged in a learning environment can be challenging and using technological resources for learning can be challenging as well.As the shift to online learning is growing more in popularity, we want to create tools that is great for learning and tracking progress. We want to help our educators pave the way for your students as they will be the main contributors for the future. And it starts with you. We wanted to create resources that can be easy to keep track of student progress as well as monitor over material and lesson plans.

For Parents

To our parents, we understand that online work and exposing your children to a different learning environment can be difficult. This can make monitoring your student’s learning easy while ensuring that they are fully engaged in a non-traditional classroom setting. You are able to keep track of their activity online and see them improve with the reading material and quizzes. It also allows collaboration with teachers for any support you or your child may need.

For Students

To our students, we wish to provide you with new learning styles in the STEM field. We’ve compiled ways for students to actively interact with the material and test their knowledge with a variety of topics in STEM. With the support from our educators and parents, we will make online learning a way that is enjoyable and educational.
